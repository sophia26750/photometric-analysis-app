Stellar Photometry & Calibration Portal

Precision tools for modern astronomical analysis.

This platform provides a complete, automated workflow for stellar photometry and magnitude calibration. Whether you're analyzing a single target or an entire star cluster, the system handles every step — from WCS solving to catalog matching, flux extraction, color‑term calibration, and final standardized magnitudes.

Built for researchers, students, and observatories, this tool transforms raw FITS images into scientifically calibrated results with clarity, transparency, and professional‑grade accuracy.

  • Analyze individual objects or full star clusters
  • Generate diagnostic plots for transparency and verification
